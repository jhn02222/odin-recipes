Lasagna

Description:

This is a recipe from allrecipes.com on how to make lasagna that will take 3 hours and 15 minutes total for a total serving size of 12.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
